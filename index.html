Project Overview

Customer Needs

Customer requires accurate measurement of plant growth via daily scans.  Integrate FarmBot and the LiDAR to make one complete system. Data must be securely stored, backed up, and easily retrievable when needed.

What we have done

Our team combined the VLP-16 LiDAR sensor with a Genesis farming robot tool called FarmBot that moves the camera around a planted area to take daily measurements.

Scope

We have integrated a VLP-16 LiDAR system with a FarmBot to monitor and analyze plant growth.  The system aims to provide real-time, accurate, and efficient data acquisition to be stored in the cloud for easy access.

Data

The team combined the VLP-16 LiDAR sensor to the FarmBot which allows the camera to move around to each of the plants tracking growth. It will take daily measurements, after the data is collected from the scan the information is sent to the Amazon Web Services S3 bucket for storage
